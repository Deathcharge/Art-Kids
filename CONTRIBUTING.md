# Contributing to Art Kids

Thank you for your interest in supporting Art Kids! There are many ways to contribute to our mission of providing quality art education to underserved students.

## Ways to Contribute

### 1. As an Art Educator or Teaching Artist

**Qualifications:**
- Experience in art education or professional art practice
- Ability to work with children and young adults
- Understanding of age-appropriate teaching methods
- Enthusiasm for making art accessible to all students

**Opportunities:**
- Teach supplemental art classes at partner schools
- Develop curriculum materials and lesson plans
- Provide professional development for other educators
- Mentor students and showcase art careers

**How to Get Started:**
- Contact us to discuss opportunities in your area
- Complete background checks and required clearances
- Participate in program orientation
- Begin making a difference in students' lives

### 2. As a School or District Partner

**Partnership Benefits:**
- Supplemental art instruction for your students
- Professional art educators at no or reduced cost
- Standards-aligned curriculum materials
- Support for building your arts program

**Requirements:**
- Commitment to regular programming schedule
- Adequate classroom space and basic supplies
- Administrative support and coordination
- Participation in program assessment

**Next Steps:**
- Contact us to discuss your school's needs
- Complete partnership application
- Work with us to design a program that fits your community
- Launch your Art Kids program

### 3. As a Financial Supporter

**Funding Needs:**
- Art supplies and materials
- Educator compensation
- Program development and curriculum creation
- Transportation and logistics
- Administrative support

**Sponsorship Levels:**
We offer various sponsorship opportunities for:
- Individual donors
- Local businesses
- Corporate partners
- Foundations and grant-makers

**Impact:**
Your support directly provides art education to students who would otherwise go without. Every contribution makes a difference.

### 4. As a Volunteer

**Volunteer Opportunities:**
- Classroom assistance
- Supply organization and inventory
- Event support for student showcases
- Administrative and operational tasks
- Marketing and outreach
- Documentation and photography

**Requirements:**
- Completion of background check
- Attendance at volunteer orientation
- Commitment to scheduled volunteer times
- Enthusiasm for supporting arts education

### 5. As a Community Partner

**Partnership Opportunities:**
- Host art exhibitions for students
- Provide workspace or studio time
- Donate art supplies or materials
- Offer field trip destinations
- Connect students with professional artists
- Sponsor special workshops or events

**Benefits:**
- Community engagement and visibility
- Tax deductible contributions
- Recognition in program materials
- Direct impact on local education

## Program Standards

All contributors to Art Kids must:
- Prioritize student safety and wellbeing
- Maintain professionalism and reliability
- Support inclusive and equitable practices
- Respect confidentiality and privacy
- Align with program values and mission

## Code of Conduct

Art Kids is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all students, educators, and partners. We expect all contributors to:

- Treat everyone with dignity and respect
- Use inclusive language and practices
- Create welcoming spaces for diverse learners
- Report concerns about safety or inappropriate behavior
- Support equity and access in all program activities

## Getting Started

Ready to contribute? Here's how:

1. **Learn More**: Review our program materials and approach
2. **Identify Your Interest**: Determine how you'd like to contribute
3. **Make Contact**: Reach out to discuss opportunities
4. **Complete Requirements**: Finish necessary paperwork, clearances, or training
5. **Get Involved**: Start making an impact in arts education!

## Questions?

We're here to help you find the right way to support Art Kids. Whether you have questions about:
- Specific volunteer opportunities
- Partnership requirements
- Donation options
- Program implementation

Please don't hesitate to reach out. Together, we can ensure every student has access to quality art education.
